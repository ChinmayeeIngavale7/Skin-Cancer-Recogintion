Skin Cancer Type

Actinic Keratosis (AKIEC)

It is also known as Intraepithelial Carcinoma. It is a type of skin lesion that can potentially become cancerous if not treated. Actinic keratosis is considered a precancerous condition, and intraepithelial carcinoma refers to cancer that is still confined within the epithelial layer of skin.

It's important to note that any type of skin lesion or condition should be evaluated and diagnosed by a qualified medical professional. If you suspect you have any skin concerns, it's recommended to consult a dermatologist for proper diagnosis and treatment.
