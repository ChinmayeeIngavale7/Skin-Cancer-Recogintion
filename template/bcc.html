Skin Cancer Type

Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC)

It is the most common type of skin cancer and typically develops in areas of the skin that are frequently exposed to the sun, such as the face, neck, and hands. Basal cell carcinoma tends to grow slowly and rarely spreads to other parts of the body.

Like other forms of skin cancer, it's crucial to get any suspicious skin growths or lesions examined by a dermatologist. Early detection and treatment of basal cell carcinoma can help prevent it from spreading and causing more serious health issues.
