Skin Cancer Type

Benign Keratosis like lesions (BKL)

It is also known as Keratotic lesions.Benign Keratosis-like lesions (BKL) are not considered dangerous. "Benign" means that these lesions are non-cancerous and do not pose a significant health risk. BKL lesions may include a variety of skin conditions that have similar characteristics to keratosis (such as scaly, rough, or thickened skin), but they are not cancerous growths.

It's important to note that a dermatologist or healthcare professional should evaluate any skin lesion to determine its nature and whether it requires further attention. While BKL lesions are typically not harmful, a medical expert's assessment can provide accurate information and guidance for appropriate management or treatment.
