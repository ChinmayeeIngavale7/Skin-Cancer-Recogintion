Skin Cancer Detection

Skin cancer is considered one of the most dangerous types of cancer and there is a drastic
increase in the rate of deaths due to lack of knowledge of the symptoms and their prevention.
This project uses machine learning and image processing tools to predict skin cancer.



About Me

The project aims to accurately classify skin lesions. Many people in rural areas are still unaware of skin cancer symptoms and fail to predict the actual problem. Using this machine learning model skin cancer prediction is possible even in rural areas. Early detection of skin cancer can lead to proper treatment which is important for successful outcomes.
