Skin Cancer Type

Dermatofibroma (DF)

Dermatofibroma is a non-cancerous skin lesion that typically appears as a firm, raised bump on the skin. It often has a reddish-brown color and can vary in size.

Dermatofibromas are usually harmless and do not require treatment unless they are causing discomfort or are cosmetically bothersome. In some cases, they may be surgically removed if they are causing symptoms or if there is concern about their appearance.

It's important to note that a dermatologist should evaluate any skin growth or lesion to determine its nature and whether any further action is needed. If you suspect you have a dermatofibroma or any other skin concern, it's recommended to seek professional medical advice.
